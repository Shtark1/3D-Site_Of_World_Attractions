Hollywood

Hollywood is an area west of downtown Los Angeles, a place famous all over the world as the cradle of American cinematography. Everything in Hollywood is connected with movie celebrities.

Colosseum

The Colosseum is one of the most recognizable architectural monuments both in Italy and around the world. For almost two thousand years, the majestic amphitheater, located in the very center of Rome, has been gathering millions of visitors who want to see this iconic structure with their own eyes.

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is the most recognizable landmark in Paris, named after its creator and receiving more than 6 million visitors annually. The tower is located in the 7th arrondissement of the French capital, in the north-western part of the Champ de Mars.

Big Ben

Big Ben is one of the most popular attractions of the capital of Great Britain and is one of the business cards of London. It is under this name that one of the three towers of the Palace of Westminster, the residence of the Parliament of the United Kingdom, located on the banks of the Thames, is known all over the world.
